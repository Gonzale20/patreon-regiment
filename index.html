Why Join Patreon?
🎖️

Recognition & Legacy

Build a permanent record of your achievements. Achievement Murals, medals, ribbons, and social media features keep your legacy alive in the Regiment.

📈

Accelerated Progression

Skip ranks, access free certifications, and train directly with the Commander. Patreon members move through the Regiment faster with exclusive advancement paths.

🎯

Exclusive Operations

Access Patreon-only missions, Game Master events, and Special Mission Operations that never open to the general membership.

🛡️

Shape the Regiment

Front row seat to leadership meetings. Your voice directly influences decisions on training, events, and Regiment direction.

🎙️

Podcast & Media Access

Participate in Regiment Podcasts, become a Patreon Streamer, and grow your gaming presence through our platform and community.

⚔️

Keep the Regiment Operational

Your support directly funds servers, tools, events, and the infrastructure that keeps the Regiment running at full capacity.

🔐

Existing Members — You're Protected

If you're currently a Sergeant-level member at the $4.99/month price point, you will be automatically upgraded to Officer Membership at the same price. No action needed. All your current benefits are preserved and you gain everything Officer has to offer.

Join before April 28th, 2025 and lock in Officer-level access permanently at the current rate.
